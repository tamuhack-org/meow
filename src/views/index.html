    WHAT WE DO

    "Hack" in hackathon is used to describe how technology can be used in new and innovative ways.


    At TAMUhack, teams spend the weekend working on innovative software and hardware solutions to real-world problems in a community full of mentorship.

    We’ll provide you with all the resources and mentors you need to work on something cool. You’ll have the freedom to create a product, learn new skills or just have fun working on a project with friends. We’ll also provide all the free food and free swag—just be sure to bring plenty of resumes for all of the awesome sponsors and mentors you’ll meet!
