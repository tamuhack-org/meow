What is HowdyHack?

HowdyHack is Texas A&M’s 24-hour, beginner friendly, Aggie-only hackathon. Hackers at HowdyHack will brainstorm, build, and break, before they’ll submit their product to a panel of judges for evaluation and hopefully win some cool prizes.

Beyond hacking, HowdyHack is about connecting creative and talented Aggie hackers with each other. We want to give hackers the creative space to showcase their skills to create something amazing while getting to know fellow hackers with the same Aggie pride!

How do I participate?

To get started, head on over to to sign up to participate. The first 100 people to sign up and then submit a project will receive a care package with a T-shirt, stickers, and other hackathon swag from our sponsors. Sign up as soon as possible to secure your spot and qualify for a care package!

After you sign up, you will receive a confirmation email letting you know you’re good to go. Now you can either get a team together or find one during the team building portion right before the competition!

What about COVID-19?

Due to concerns about spreading of COVID-19, this year’s HowdyHack will be hosted online but that does not mean it will be any less exciting! The hackathon will be streamed on Twitch with lots of workshops, events, and interactive competitions to help create the engaging atmosphere that comes with in-person hackathons.
