    1. TAMUhack 2014

      A new tradition starts in (Room Held??) on a warm October weekend. 50 students spent a weekend creating projects and having fun.

    2. TAMUhack 2015

      New location, new design, we’re making things bigger. The second TAMUhack brought over 200 students and 40 projects.

    3. TAMUhack 2016

      Polka dots and pastels make their first appearance.

    4. TAMUhack 2018

      Even the best traditions need a little change. Switching from a Fall hackathon to a Spring hackathon.

    5. HowdyHack 2018

      The first iteration of HowdyHack. Exclusive, aggie-only hackathon.

    6. TAMUhack 2019

      Cue celebrations, our 5th anniversary of TAMUhack. We had our largest TAMUhack yet with 800 participants and 120 projects submitted! We introduced the TH lighted sign that has become a staple at our events!

    7. HowdyHack 2019

      Our fall hackathon nearly triples in size! Over 200 aggies coding the night away with a bunch of activities and mini-events.
